About

The path we traversed

  • 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Started as a sole proprietorship company to design and manufacture sine inverters and Li-ion Batteries.

  • 2021

    SE MOZ is Born

    we built our first E-Bike SE MOZ. The product first revealed  at Start-Up Expo conducted by Kerala Start-Up Mission 

  • MARCH 2022

    Transition to Sign Eminent Pvt. Ltd

    In March 2022 the company registered as a Private Limited company under the name Sign Eminent Enterprises Pvt. Ltd

  • July 2022

    Launch of SE Ragna

    Announced our next generation Sporty E-bike SE Ragna. Proposed to launch on early 2023

SE MOZ

Customized E-Bike with 60 km range under ₹ 45000 

SE MOZ is one of the first E-Bike in India which facilites infotainment system onboard.
It also has a longer mileage and a better performance compared to it's competitors.

SE HYB

Converted 50+ IC engine bikes.

We convert existing IC engine bikes and four wheelers into hybrid electric vehicles which has higher fuel efficiency and increased mileage.
We use Indigenously developed BLDC hub motor and controller for the electric drive.

Battery Packs

Customized Li-ion battery pack

We provide customized Li-ion battery pack for solar applications as well as electric vehicle applications.
Both Li-ion and LiFePO4 cells are directly imported from china and tested from our laboratries.
We also provide extended warranties for selected range of specifications.
